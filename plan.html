Goals

The goal for the 'cossieplay' website is essentially to increase local customer traffic in store. Hopefully increasing the money that 'cossieplay' makes.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be evaluated by monitoring the in store customer traffic and/or the ammount of money the store is making.

Target Audience

As stated in the client brief the target audience is generally going to be males/females between the ages 15-20 (new customers) and 35-50. (most frequent customer age) Generally the target audience for any cosplay is geeky.

In order to appeal to this target audience the website should show various video game/sci fi/fantasy themes. Seing as the target audience will range from 15-50, both old (eg. zelda, mario) and new (eg. WoW, League of Legends) themes should be used.
